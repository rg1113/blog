About


This is me...

I am a wife and mother of two, my family is what motivates me to wake up with a new sense of determination and without them I would not be where I am today. I am encouraged on a daily basis to pursue my passion and I can honestly say I am extremely lucky in that sense.

My passion for learning has brought me to a new challenge which I will call the intricacies of learning how to code. My journey for the most part has had a lot of ups and downs and as with learning anything new, it has its frustrating moments.

With that being said I have never really felt as purposeful as when I am coding, I can honestly tell you I have written and re-written the same string of code in different manners just to achieve the task I set out for it to do, there has been a lot of trial and error … and more error.
The key is learning from these mistakes, and keep up your work because as I have come to learn you can ask your website to do the same thing a thousand different ways by simply writing a new code.
